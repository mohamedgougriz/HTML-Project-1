Resume

Bill Gates is an American business magnate, software developer, and philanthropist. He co-founded Microsoft in 1975, leading the company to dominate the personal computer industry with Windows. Known for his innovation and strategic vision, Gates became one of the richest people in the world. In 2000, he founded the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, focusing on global health, education, and poverty reduction. Today, he is recognized as both a technology pioneer and a leading humanitarian.
